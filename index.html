FlipIt

A fresh and fun way to flip through flyers of campus events.

Visual

We aim to change the way you look at a flyer to change the way we look for events. Explore our sleek, simple interface design and keep up to date with what's happening on campus.

Virtual

Guilty of throwing away paper flyers? So are we. Let's turn the tables and save some paper. Promoters save printing money and we save waste. It's a win-win situation.

Vocal

Want students to hear about your event? Simply click the Add Event button to post your flyer. It's as easy as that!
